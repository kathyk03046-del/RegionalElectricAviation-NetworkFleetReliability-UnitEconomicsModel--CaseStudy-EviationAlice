# Regional Electric Aviation: Fleet Reliability & Unit Economics Model
## Case Study: Eviation Alice Propulsion & Battery Telemetry

**#Aircraft Physics** **#Fleet Economics**. 

### Project Goal
To evaluate the operational boundaries of the Eviation Alice (Cargo Variant) by analyzing high-frequency battery telemetry across 100+ simulated missions.

### Key Business Questions Addressed:
* **Energy-Payload Efficiency:** How do payload mass and ambient temperature impact energy consumption per mile?
* **Predictive Maintenance:** Can we identify early signs of battery degradation through discharge curve anomalies?
* **Operational ROI:** Finding the optimal balance between rapid charging (turnaround time) and battery cycle life longevity.

### Tech Stack:
* **Python (Pandas/NumPy):** Physics-based telemetry simulation and data cleansing.
* **SQL:** Relational data modeling and cohort analysis.
* **Excel:** Strategic ROI and Sensitivity modeling (Goal Seek/Solver).
* **Tableau:** Executive decision dashboards.
